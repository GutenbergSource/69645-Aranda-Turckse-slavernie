Over dit werk

Emanuel De Airanda (1614-1686) was een Zuid-Nederlandse reiziger, historieschrijver en dichter. Hij verbleef twee jaar in slavernij in Algiers. Bij terugkomst schreef hij dit verhaal op. Eerst in het Frans, maar een paar jaar later ook in deze Nederlandse vertaling.

Proeflezen

About this work

Emanuel De Airanda (1614-1686) was a Southern Netherlands traveller, historian and poet. He lived for two years in slavery in Algeria. On his return, he published a book on his experiences. Initially written in French, but published in this Dutch translation a few years later.

Proofreading

The long s (ſ) has been added as a custom character to this project. It can be put in directly. Note that both 'w' and 'vv' (double 'v') are used. Please retain the use of 'vv' as printed.
